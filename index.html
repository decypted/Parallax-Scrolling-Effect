Anatomy of the Great Zimbabwe Empire

A Brief Overview

The Great Zimbabwe was an ancient frican city known for its large circular wall and tower, as depicted in the image shown. It was part of a wealthy African trading empire that controlled much of the East African coast from the 11th to the 15th centuries C.E.
